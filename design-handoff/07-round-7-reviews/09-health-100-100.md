## Step 2 — Polish closures (six items shipped this run)

### Closure 1: F108 DIE_SHOULDER_PAIN + DIE_FLANK_PAIN auto-detection
**File:** `crisis-service.jsx`
**Was:** Both rules existed in `ENDO_SAFETY_RULES` but `assessEndoSafetyRules()` had no detection helpers — they could only fire from manual reporting.
**Now:** Added `detectCyclicalBodyMapZone(painLocationLog, zoneId, minSeverity, minCycles, cycleLen)` helper. Wired:
- `DIE_SHOULDER_PAIN` fires when body-map zone `right_shoulder` shows NRS ≥4 in menstrual phase across ≥2 distinct cycles
- `DIE_FLANK_PAIN` fires when zones `lower_back` or `flank` show NRS ≥4 in menstrual phase across ≥2 cycles
**Clinical meaning:** Sarah persona's "I cried, four doctors missed it" moment now extends to thoracic + ureteral DIE patterns, not just bowel/bladder.
**Closes:** -8 Functional gap (the single biggest health-score deduction from the QA audit).

### Closure 2: TimeBadge wired into ADHD-RS + WFIRS-S
**File:** `modules-5-adhd.jsx` `M.adhdRs` and `M.wfirs`
**Was:** TimeBadge primitive existed but only EHP-30 used it.
**Now:** ADHD-RS shows "Section 1 of 1 · ~5 min"; WFIRS-S shows "Section 1 of 6 · ~8 min". Users see the time commitment up front; abandonment prevention.
**Closes:** Sana's P0-12 polish item.

### Closure 3: M.exec + M.adhdMed empty-state copy
**File:** `modules-3.jsx`
**Was:** When zero ADHD daily logs existed, M.exec showed "—/10" across 5 dimensions; M.adhdMed showed empty phase boxes. Users had to infer the empty state from the dashes.
**Now:** Both modules check `dates.length === 0` and render dedicated empty states with EmptyArt illustration + clear next-action copy ("Log a day to see your average. Open Tools → ADHD → Daily ADHD log.").
**Closes:** Sana's P0-13 polish item.

### Closure 4: F138 ADHD physician report — milestone progress bar
**File:** `modules-5-adhd.jsx` `M.adhdPhysicianReportReal`
**Was:** Showed "{N} days of data ready. Cycle correlation chart unlocks at 60 days." in flat text.
**Now:** Visual progress bar (sage → eucalyptus gradient) showing days/90 + named milestones at day 7 (pattern emerging), 30 (single-cycle baseline), 60 (cycle correlation chart unlocks F134), 90 (two-cycle clinical baseline). Replicates the F134 commitment-device pattern Iris flagged.
**Closes:** Iris's product-enhancement Q3 ask.

### Closure 5: ShareInsight primitive replaces screenshot-as-workaround
**File:** `shared.jsx` (new component, exported via `window.HQ.ShareInsight`)
**Was:** Sofia mentioned "screenshot" three times across her journey — she was using the OS screenshot tool to send insights to her therapist.
**Now:** `<ShareInsight title body label />` component renders a small action row with Copy (clipboard.writeText) and Email (mailto:) actions. Wired into F134 hormonal-ADHD engine insight cards as the first surface — when the cycle correlation engine fires an insight, user gets a "Send to provider · copy / email" footer.
**Closes:** Iris's product-question Q3 ask.

### Closure 6: state.labVault → state.labs naming consistency
**File:** `modules-6-peri.jsx` `M.cvDash`
**Was:** F78 Cardiovascular Risk Dashboard read from `state.labVault` which is never initialized; the `|| []` fallback hid the break, so users with lab data still saw "no lab data."
**Now:** Reads from `state.labs` (the existing convention used by M.labVault + 7 other PCOS modules).
**Closes:** The Phase A.2 integration bug found earlier in this QA run.
